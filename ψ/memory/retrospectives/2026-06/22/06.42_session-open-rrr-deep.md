## Honest Feedback (3 friction points)

**1. Fleet registry staleness is a recurrent failure pattern, not a one-time bug.**
The rule to update the registry at every /rrr close was written June 8. It was immediately not followed for the next 14 days across 3+ implied sessions. The issue is structural: the /rrr skill doesn't read or update the registry automatically. The rule depends on the AI remembering to do it during /rrr. That is a weak enforcement mechanism. Fix: build the registry update into the /rrr flow itself, not as a learning to be remembered.
